About Me

I thrive at creating unique designs utilizing my knowledge of best-in-class software and varied experience in both print and digital mediums. Translating customer requirements into an impeccable finished product is the most satisfying part of my job.

Technology is central to my creative process. Understanding what I can do and what I need to learn in a big part of what moves me forward with any project I take on. The scope of any project is the first thing I make a point defining so I know how to best navigate it. Constantly asking "What Skills do I have so I can get started?" or "What must I learn to complete the project?" Learning new technology and new techniques is very important to me as the industry grows and evolves. Whether it be video, photography, or graphic design; I do quality work that meets and exceeds customer expectations.
