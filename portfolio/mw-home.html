What is Mentally_Wrong?

Mentally_Wrong is the personal side project of Bryan Millett to explore the art and design of enterainment media without the traditional corporate mentality. This personal side project is a way to show the world the best work is done when great minds work seperate from corporate minds. We explore the art and design of media while soliving problems in the real world through design. We build communities on Twitch to escape the real world and have a digital oasis away from the horrors of reality. We ponder and explore the merits and influence of games while celebrating the medium on Youtube. We solve modern day issues for everyone who needs the help in the real world. Mentally_Wrong is about taking a stand against corporate culture and helping those who needs that extra hand in their day-to-day life.
