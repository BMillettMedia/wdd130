Arizona

Arizona is known for its amazing cultural influences all across the state. Known for the extreme yet dry heat, it boasts its rich culture with various musemums dedicated to the arts like the Musical Instrument Museum, also known as the MIM. If you can manage the heat, Arizona is known for its amazing hiking trails off of Camelback Mountain or in the Sonoran Desert Preseve. This makes it a wonder place to take gorgeous sunset shots for your instagram and social media.
