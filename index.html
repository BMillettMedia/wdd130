Arizona

Arizona is a state in the United States. It is home to wonderful landmarks and amazing city life. Places like Tempe, home of Arizona State University, Glendale with its wonderful entertainment district of WestGate, and Flagstaff, home of the Grand Canyon. Often known for the 120-degree weather, it is surprising to know that it is capable of snowing in the northern mountain ranges of Arizona and in the south by Tucson.
